What is PREVENT Ireland?

With the rise of extremist and xenophobic behaviour both on and offline,
there is no better time to arm yourself with the knowledge needed
to recognise and protect those most vulnerable from the effects of radicalization.

Our aim is enable you to arm you with the information you seek and enable you to find the right help where needed.
